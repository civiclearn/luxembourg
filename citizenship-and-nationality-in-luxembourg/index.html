Nationality and citizenship: basic concepts

Nationality is the legal bond between a person and a state.

Citizenship grants full political rights, including voting in national elections.

Luxembourg nationality

Luxembourg nationality defines who is considered a citizen of the Grand Duchy.

Citizens enjoy specific rights and are subject to certain civic duties.

Ways of acquiring Luxembourg nationality

Luxembourg nationality can be acquired in several ways, including by birth, descent, or naturalisation.

Each pathway is governed by legal conditions defined by law.

Naturalisation

Naturalisation allows foreign residents to become Luxembourg citizens.

It requires fulfilling conditions related to residence, language knowledge, and civic integration.

Rights linked to citizenship

Luxembourg citizens have full political rights, including voting and standing for election at national level.

Citizenship also strengthens participation in democratic life.

Citizenship and living together

Citizenship reflects integration into Luxembourg society and its values.

It plays an important role in social cohesion and the concept of vivre-ensemble.
