What does “vivre-ensemble” mean?

“Vivre-ensemble” refers to the ability of people from different backgrounds to live together peacefully within the same society.

It emphasises mutual respect, dialogue, and shared rules.

A diverse society

Luxembourg is characterised by cultural, linguistic, and social diversity.

Living together requires recognising and respecting these differences.

Shared values and rules

Vivre-ensemble is based on common values such as respect for the law, equality, and fundamental rights.

These principles apply to all residents, regardless of origin.

Respect and responsibility

Living together implies respecting others and their freedoms.

It also involves taking responsibility for one’s actions within society.

The role of institutions

Public institutions promote vivre-ensemble through laws, education, and integration policies.

They help ensure social cohesion and peaceful coexistence.

Why this topic matters for the Vivre-Ensemble test

The Vivre-Ensemble test evaluates understanding of social values and civic life.

Knowing the meaning of vivre-ensemble is central to the test’s objectives.
