What is Luxembourg National Day?

Luxembourg National Day is the official national holiday of the Grand Duchy.

It is celebrated every year on 23 June.

The origin of the National Day

The date officially marks the birthday of the Grand Duke.

Although the Grand Duke’s actual birthday may differ, 23 June was chosen for practical and historical reasons.

Official ceremonies

National Day includes official ceremonies attended by the Grand Duke, members of the government, and public authorities.

These ceremonies symbolise state continuity and national unity.

Public celebrations

Public festivities take place across the country, especially in Luxembourg City.

They include concerts, parades, fireworks, and community events.

The role of traditions

Traditions associated with National Day bring together residents of different backgrounds.

They reinforce a shared sense of belonging and vivre-ensemble.
