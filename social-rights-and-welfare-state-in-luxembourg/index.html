How social protection supports cohesion and solidarity in Luxembourg.

Understand social protection in Luxembourg

Social rights are a key pillar of civic life and social cohesion.

What are social rights?

Social rights are rights that guarantee access to essential social protection and services.

They aim to ensure a dignified standard of living and social security for all.

The foundations of the welfare state

Luxembourg has developed a strong welfare state based on solidarity and social insurance.

Employees, employers, and the state contribute to financing social protection.

Social security and healthcare

The social security system covers healthcare, sickness benefits, pensions, and family allowances.

Access to healthcare is a central element of social rights in Luxembourg.

Support for families and workers

Family benefits, parental leave, and unemployment support help protect individuals against social risks.

These measures promote social stability and equal opportunities.

Social assistance and inclusion

Social assistance supports people facing financial difficulties or social exclusion.

The goal is to promote inclusion and participation in society.
